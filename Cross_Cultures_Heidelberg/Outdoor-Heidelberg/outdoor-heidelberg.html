Presentation:

Simple and Friendly

We hike or cycle on some sundays for a few hours in the Heidelberg region, eat and drink together, chat, and return home satisfied. No membership required.

We are a non-profit interest community (similar to a private group of friends).

We may also plan a canoe trip in the summer, but this is not yet decided.


Concept

We organize events on some weekends. Registration is easy via a short email (see the "Contact" page).

You will then receive the meeting point by email. All explanations from the organizing team are in English,

but in small groups you can speak any language you like. Ideal for foreigners who want to integrate, learn German,

and make new friends. Also ideal for Germans who speak other languages ​​and want to experience new cultures. We have participants of all ages.



Rules

By registering for an event or participating, you agree to our rules. Please read them carefully in the section "Our Rules".

Events


Applies to all events: To register for an event, simply send us a brief email (see contact page). By registering, you automatically accept our rules. Please read the "Our Rules" page carefully. The meeting point will then be sent to registered participants via email.

Please note that the safety of every participant is our highest priority. Therefore, events may be canceled at short notice (1-2 days in advance) if the weather or other conditions are dangerous or unpleasant, or if there are insufficient participants. In this case, we will notify all registered participants by email and also announce it here on this website.

Participation is free of charge. However, we would appreciate a small donation (e.g., €2 per participant) to help cover our website and advertising costs.

Our Rules:


With registrating or participating, you agree to follow our rules. We reserve the right to exclude anyone who does not comply or who acts against the group's best interest.

- Treat all participants with respect regardless of race, gender, nationality, sexual orientation or any other characteristics

- Do NOT take pictures of other people without their consent.

- Do NOT post any photos from our events online or on social media. We also will not post them.

- Avoid canceling your participation on short notice unless you have a genuine emergency or are ill.

- Do not participate if your health does not permit it.

- Behave always carefully and do not put at risk other persons or yourself.

- Always wear appropriate clothing and shoe. If needed, bring protective glasses or sun cream...etc... Also carry enough water and food for the event.

- For organizational and safety reasons, we may have a limited number of seats.

- In case of an accident or serious injury, we will call local emergency services immediately. For other issue, always carry with you the telephone number of a trusted person who can help. If you can not or do not want to continue and are able to get home on your own, you may walk to the nearest bus and train station and use public transportations to go home.

- for cycling events: cycling involve risks for yourself and others. You should have experience cycling on both dry and wet roads. Wet roads are more dangerous with a higher risk of sliping and reduced brake performance. Ensure that you can adapt safely. Know and follow all german traffic rules. Make sure your bike is in good condition: tyres properly inflated, brakes working well. Helmets are strongly recommended for your safety.

Important: Our community assumes no liability for accidents/sickness or damages. All participants are responsible for having their own health and liability insurance (Krankenversicherung and Haftpflichversicherung) which should be valid in Germany. Participation is at your own risk, and all applicable laws must be followed.
